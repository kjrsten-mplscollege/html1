David Bowie

David Robert Jones (8 January 1947 - 10 January 2016), known professionally as David Bowie, was an English singer, songwriter and actor. Regarded as one of the most influential musicians of the 20th century, Bowie was acclaimed by critics and musicians, particularly for his innovative work during the 1970s. His 50-year career was marked by reinvention and visual presentation, and his music and stagecraft have had a great impact on popular music.
